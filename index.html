I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in Electrical Engineering from Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology, Vietnam, in 2000 and 2005, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from The University of Texas at Arlington in 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Hao Che. I joined Prof. Hong Jiang and Prof. Hao Che's lab as a Postdoctoral Research Associate from Aug 2017 and started working as Sr. Hardware Integration Engineer at Ikon Technologies from Aug 2019.
